Who We Are

More Than a Church.
We're Family.

At Church 244, everything we do centers around Jesus Christ and the truth of God's Word. We're a growing community in Richland, Mississippi, and our doors are always open.

Rooted in Scripture

We believe in one true God, eternally existing in three persons — Father, Son, and Holy Spirit. The Bible is our foundation for all we do.

Built on Love

We're not just a Sunday gathering — we're a family that walks through life together. You'll find genuine connection and people who truly care.

Spirit-Led Worship

Our worship isn't a performance — it's a posture. Come expecting to encounter God's presence in a real and powerful way every single service.

Our Pastor

Josh Watts

"I love this place and these people. They are like my family! Pastor Josh Watts is a mighty man of God. He incorporates the Lord into every aspect of life and is a great leader who follows Jesus and leads others to Him."

Pastor Josh is a devoted husband to Jeanette, father to Joshua and Alana, and "Pop" to Ripley. His heart burns for one thing — helping people encounter the living God and discover their purpose in Jesus Christ.

Under his leadership, Church 244 has become a place where lives are transformed, families are restored, and the community is impacted with the love of Christ.
